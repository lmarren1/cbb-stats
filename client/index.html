The Problem

Understanding the specific combination of players and plays to pit against opponents is the age-old problem in basketball coaching.

Classic basketball analytics place too much emphasis on abstract boxscore-derived statistics to solve this issue. Such analyses lack the depth necessary to capture more than just the 'what' that occurs underneath play-heavy, fast-paced college basketball.

Inspired by modern approaches, I built Play Theory to uncover why certain players, lineups, and plays succeed against specific opponents.

I believe coaches, staff members, and players may find my software particularly helpful for improving play-calling, scouting, and individual/team development.

Your New Analytics Solution

Championships are not built on box-scores.

They're painstakingly fought for every posession, during every play-call, by each player on the floor.

Play Theory breaks down games using SideArm Sports play-by-play data and compiles it back into actionable, interactive insights on player, lineup, and play performance for coaches and players alike.

How It Works

Play Theory integrates:

  • An efficient PostgreSQL database
  • An asynchronous Node.js backend
  • And customizable Plotly.js frontend visuals

all wrapped in a lightning-fast, modern web interface.
